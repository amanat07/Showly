Our Story

Showly was born from a simple idea: to make movie-going a seamless and enjoyable experience. As three second-year Computer Science students at Chitkara University, we were tired of the hassle of booking tickets and wanted to create a platform that was not only functional but also visually stunning. We're a team passionate about technology, design, and, most importantly, movies.

This project is our way of combining our academic knowledge with our love for cinema. Every line of code and every design choice is a reflection of our commitment to creating a product that we would love to use ourselves.
