Refund & Cancellation Policy

Ticket Cancellation & Refund Eligibility

At **SHOWLY**, we understand that plans can change. We offer users the ability to cancel tickets and request a refund, subject to the following terms and conditions:

  • **User-Initiated Cancellation:** Users may cancel their movie tickets up to **2 hours before the showtime**. A refund will be processed after deducting a minimal cancellation fee.
  • **Show Cancellation:** If the movie show is cancelled by the cinema or distributor, users are eligible for a **full refund** without any deductions.
  • **Technical Errors:** If payment is deducted but the ticket is not issued, the full amount will be automatically refunded.
  • **Duplicate Booking:** If the same ticket is booked more than once due to a technical issue, one of the transactions will be refunded in full.

Cancellation Charges

In case of voluntary user cancellations, a small cancellation fee will apply to cover payment gateway and processing charges. **The exact deduction amount will be displayed at the time of cancellation.**

Refund Processing Time

Refunds will be credited back to the original mode of payment. Depending on your bank or payment provider, this may take **5 to 10 working days**.
