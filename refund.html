Refund & Cancellation Policy

Ticket Cancellation & Refunds

Once a movie ticket is successfully purchased on **SHOWLY**, it is generally considered **non-refundable and non-cancellable** except under the specific conditions listed below.

  • **Show Cancellation:** If the movie show is officially cancelled by the cinema, a full refund for the ticket price will be processed automatically within 5-7 business days.
  • **Technical Errors:** If an amount was debited from your account but the ticket was not issued due to a technical error, the transaction will be reversed, and a refund will be initiated immediately.
  • **Duplicate Transaction:** In case of a duplicate successful payment for the same booking, one of the transactions will be fully refunded.

Processing Time

For all valid refunds, the amount will be credited back to the original payment source. The refund process typically takes **7 to 10 working days** to reflect in your bank account, depending on your bank's processing time.
